# Where Managers Waste Time – Interactive Simulation

Most managers are busy.

But busy does not equal effective.

This simulation exposes a critical gap:
👉 Managers don’t know where their time actually goes — and what it’s costing their team.

---

## 🎯 The Real Problem

In most organizations, managers spend majority of their time on:

- Meetings  
- Reporting  
- Firefighting  

While under-investing in:

- Coaching  
- Strategic thinking  
- Team development  

---

## ⚠️ Why This Matters

This imbalance leads to:

- Declining team productivity  
- High dependency on managers  
- Slower decision-making  
- Increased burnout and disengagement  

👉 The issue is not effort — it’s misallocation of time.

---

## 💡 What This Tool Does

This is not a quiz or survey.

It is a **decision-based simulation** where you:

- Navigate a full working day as a manager  
- Make real-world decisions across 7 scenarios  
- Operate within an 8-hour time constraint  

---

## 🧠 What You Discover

At the end, you get:

- 📊 Your actual time distribution  
- 🔴 Your leadership pattern (reactive vs strategic)  
- 🟡 Business impact of your choices  
- 🟢 Clear actions to improve  

---

## 🚀 Why This Is Different

- No forms  
- No data collection  
- No theoretical questions  

👉 Just real decisions and real consequences
